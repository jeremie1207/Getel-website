Health and safety policy at work

GETEL is a SARL whose mission is to carry out preventive and corrective maintenance as well as services in the field of telecommunications while taking into account aspects related to health and safety at work. With reference to our mission statement, Management is fully committed to constantly improving health and safety by implementing a health and safety management system based on the OHSA 18001 (V.2007) standard. We will ensure that: We comply with legal and other subscribed requirements applicable to occupational health and safety; We maintain the health and safety of staff, as well as that of our visitors and subcontractors through a prevention policy, by creating an adequate working environment; Work processes are optimized so as to reduce risks; We develop the skills of our staff through an effective training program; To effectively achieve its health and safety objectives, Management undertakes to: Conduct a permanent awareness campaign to obtain the support of all employees in order to build and establish a strong spirit of occupational health and safety within GETEL. Provide a framework for the establishment and review of occupational health and safety objectives and targets; Conduct and use management reviews to assess progress and trigger continuous improvement activities; This policy will be translated into objectives throughout the organization; it will apply to all employees, subcontractors and all stakeholders who carry out activities in the sites and facilities under the responsibility of GETEL SARL. The Management delegates the mission of total health and safety management to SHEQ MANA-GER (Quality, Safety and Environment) which is responsible for implementing and maintaining the occupational health and safety management system
